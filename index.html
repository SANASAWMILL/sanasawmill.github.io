Our Story

Honest teak. Skilled cuts. Built on trust.

Sana Saw Mill, established in 2012, has built a strong reputation as a trusted dealer in Indian teak wood. We specialize in supplying high-quality sawn sizes of Adilabad and Ballarshah teak at competitive and reasonable prices.

We source ethically harvested, well-seasoned teak and other premium Indian hardwoods, processed using a thoughtful blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern precision machinery. Carpenters, contractors, and families across Telangana rely on us -for projects of every scale, from a single beam to complete housing requirements.

What We Offer
